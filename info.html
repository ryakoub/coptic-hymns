Curriculum

The hymns' curriculum closely follows the Mahragan Al-Keraza 2026 curriculum as designed by Egypt with a few adjustments to fit the timeline of the Summer break. Also, since Egypt doesn't have a specific curriculum for college and graduates for 2026, a list from previous years is assembled to serve the needs of our churches.

Level 1 Rules

As usual, Level 1 is for a group of learners that need to learn together and present the hymn as harmonized choir. While the preferred hymns language is Coptic, it is up to the church to decide if they want to learn it in English. English source is not provided as there is no standardized version. Additionally, in previous years, provided audio sources were not followed by some churches.

Level 2 Rules

Level 2 is for individual learners who want to learn the hymn on their own and present it as a soloist. Level 2 hymns must be in Coptic, if presented in English, it will be disregarded.

Submission Rules

Similar to previous years, video recordings will be required for submission. Details will be provided later.

How to Use Coptic Chanter

Coptic Chanter is an interactive tool designed to help you learn Coptic hymns with synchronized audio and lyrics. Here's how to make the most of it:

Audio Synchronization

The audio is synchronized with the lyrics at different levels of detail to suit your learning style:

  • By Word: The audio highlights each word as it's sung, allowing you to follow along precisely and match pronunciation.
  • By Stanza: The entire stanza highlights together, helping you understand phrase grouping and flow.
  • By Breaths: Marks natural breathing points in the hymn, useful for learning proper phrasing and where to breathe.
  • By Verse: Highlights complete verses, useful for understanding larger sections of the hymn.

Available Options

Customize your learning experience through various settings:

  • Sync Level: Set the "Repeat Verse" to on if you want the current verse to repeat automatically.
  • Highlighting: Toggle the auto scroll feature to keep the current verse in view as the audio plays.
  • Font Selection: Switch between modern and traditional Coptic fonts based on your preference.
  • Playback Speed: Adjust the audio playback speed to practice at your own pace.
  • Pulse Animation: Enable or disable visual pulsing during playback.

Interactive Word Selection

Click on any word in the lyrics to jump to that exact point in the audio. This is helpful for:

  • Practicing specific difficult words or phrases
  • Repeating sections you want to improve
  • Understanding pronunciation of individual words
